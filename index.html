What is the Anthropocene?

The Earth is 4.5 billion years old, and humanity has been around for a mere 200,000 years. Yet in that time we have manipulated and changed the environment for the sake of survival. The environment has gotten worse as we continue to cut down forests, burn fossil fuels, and the pollute the planet with plastic. Humanity’s impact on the planet has pushed Earth into a new geological epoch—the Anthropocene.
